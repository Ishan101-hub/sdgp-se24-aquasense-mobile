## LinkedIn Team Launch Post

We are excited to present **AquaSense** — an IoT-based smart multi-zone water monitoring and leakage detection system developed by Blue Squad SE-24.

AquaSense helps households monitor real-time water usage, detect leakages at zone level, and remotely control water valves through a Flutter application. The system integrates ESP32 microcontrollers, flow sensors, solenoid valves, MQTT communication, a FastAPI backend, Supabase PostgreSQL, and Firebase Hosting.

Live Demo: https://aquasense-sdgp.web.app
